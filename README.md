## Datasets Used
We used the following datasets from the Western Pennsylvania Regional Data Center (WPRDC) to evaluate our overarching metric of "bestness":

1. **Monthly Criminal Activity**
   * **Description:** This dataset includes Pittsburgh Bureau of Police crime incidents.
   * **link:** https://data.wprdc.org/zh_Hans_CN/dataset/monthly-criminal-activity-dashboard

2. **Pittsburgh Parking Meters and Payment Points**
   * **Description:** This dataset contains information on the Pittsburgh Parking Authority's parking meters and other payments points, including a description of the location of the meter, which parking zone it is in, and its geocoordinates.
   * **Link:** https://data.wprdc.org/dataset/pittsburgh-parking-meters-and-payment-points#

3. **City of Pittsburgh Trees**
   * **Description:** This dataset contains trees maintained by the City of Pittsburgh and includes neighborhood information and estimated environmental benefits such as stormwater, air quality, and carbon-related benefits.
   * **Link:** https://data.wprdc.org/dataset/city-trees
